Built for schools

Designed around student safety from day one

UK secondary schools have demanding data protection requirements. Cronkite is architected around those requirements, not retrofitted to them. Every decision — from how students log in to how AI analysis is generated — was made with safeguarding and GDPR compliance in mind.

🔐

UK GDPR Compliant

Student data is stored in UK-based infrastructure. No student data is shared with third parties or used to train AI models.

👤

Google OAuth Login

Students log in with existing school Google accounts. No passwords to manage, no new accounts to create.

🛡️

Safeguarding-First Architecture

AI sessions are single-turn with no persistent memory of student interactions. No AI chatbot access for students.

📋

DPA Available on Request

A full Data Processing Agreement and DPIA are available for school data protection leads and IT managers.
